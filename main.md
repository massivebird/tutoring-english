# Phrases / Idioms

## Fundamental

Appropriate for all types of conversation.

+ Casual vs formal
+ Lucky vs fortunate (and their opposites)
+ Sophisticated (try finding etymology)
+ All around
+ Worth a shot
+ [not] in the mood
+ I can't stand this
+ Look it up
+ To be pissed
+ It slipped my mind
+ Get a move on

## Comfortable

Casual language. Widely understood.

+ That's crazy
+ My jam
+ That's sick (and sickening is just bad)
+ I'm down
+ It's bananas

## Exotic

Very rare.

+ Take a deuce
